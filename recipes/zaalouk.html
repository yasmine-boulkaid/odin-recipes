zaalouk

description

zaalouk is a traditional moroccan dish. it is made with aubergine, tomatoe, garlic and a numeber of spices. it is enjoyed with bread

ingredients

  • 2 aubergines
  • 2 tomatoes
  • garlic to taste
  • salt, pepper, paprika and cumin to taste
  • oil for cooking

steps

  1. peel and cut the aubergines into small cubes.
  2. put oil in a pot and cook the auergines over medium-low heat. cover.
  3. grate the tomatoes and finely chop the garlic.
  4. cook them in a seperate pan. cook on medium-high heat until all water is evaporated.
  5. when everything is cooked take off the heat, combine and season.
  6. enjoy with bread!
