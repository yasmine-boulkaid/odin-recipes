l3dss

description

l3dss is a traditional moroccan dish. it is a lentil soup/stew. it can be enjoyed with bread.

ingredients

  • 500g green lentils
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • garlic to taste
  • salt, pepper, paprika and cumin to taste
  • oil for cooking

steps

  1. soak the lentils. this is optional but the longer they soak, the quicker they will cook.
  2. grate the tomatoes and onions.
  3. finely chop the garlic.
  4. put everything into a pan with enough water to cover it. add every spice except for cumin.
  5. add water if necessary.
  6. when cooked take off the heat and add the cumin.
  7. enjoy in a bowl with small pieces bread!
